About the Book

The CompTIA A+ Cyber Study Guide, 2026 Edition is the definitive companion for the CompTIA A+ Cyber course — a beginner-friendly, fast-track program designed to build foundational cybersecurity knowledge and earn your CompCert (Competency Certificate).

Written by Eric Tomchik, this 227-page guide covers all nine A+ Cyber domains with in-depth explanations, real-world scenarios, hands-on lab exercises, and a full 110-question practice assessment.

About the Author

Eric Tomchik

Eric Tomchik is a cybersecurity professional and entrepreneur with deep expertise in Linux system administration, network security, and IT infrastructure. He holds CompTIA A+, CompTIA Network+ (CompTIA CIOS), Microsoft AZ-900, Microsoft SC-900, and LPI Linux Essentials certifications. Eric is passionate about making cybersecurity education accessible to everyone and dedicated to staying current with the latest developments in the field.
